Stargo Connect combines web technologies and responsive designs to let you offer a branded, social online event experience to your guests on their desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. Host a global, high quality virtual event with rich media, video, and social interactions, all delivered to you as a service, eliminating the need for installation and implementation. Stargo Connect allows you to focus on reaching your guests while gaining valuable, real-time insight to marketing effectiveness.
