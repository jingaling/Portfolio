Fun Fa'n is a website that provides the introduction of rice festivals and the platform for users to share their rice stories. In this porject, users can know about the origin of rice as different foods in the specific festival as well as get the latest news to join in the activities while they are in Taiwan. In addition, they can explore rice stories or connect with Facebook to share their experiences about joining in this culture.
